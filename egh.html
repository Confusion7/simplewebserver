Ratan Tata



Ratan Naval Tata (born 28 December 1937) is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist and former chairman of Tata Sons. He was a chairman of the Tata Group from 1990 to 2012, and interim chairman from October 2016 through February 2017. He continues to head its charitable trusts.In 2008, he received the Padma Vibhushan, the second highest civilian honour in India, after receiving the Padma Bhushan, the third highest civilian honour in 2000.


Transforming the Tata Group:

Growth and Expansion: Under his leadership, the Tata Group witnessed significant growth and diversification, venturing into new sectors like IT, telecommunications, and automobiles. The iconic acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover in 2008 marked a bold step onto the global stage. Focus on Innovation: He championed innovation and technological advancements, driving the group's success in the face of globalization.
